# Mood-menu-cafe
Mood Menu Café is not just a place to eat and drink—it’s an experience designed to match your emotions. Whether you’re feeling happy, relaxed, romantic, or even a little angry, our café offers a unique menu and curated music to complement your mood.
- Personalized Atmosphere: Customers begin by selecting their mood from our interactive menu. Each mood unlocks a tailored selection of food, drinks, and songs that perfectly fit the vibe.
- Mood-Based Menus:
- Angry Mood – Bold, spicy flavors and fiery drinks to match your intensity.
- Happy Mood – Bright, colorful treats and refreshing beverages to keep the joy flowing.
- Romantic Mood – Sweet, indulgent desserts and cozy drinks for special moments.
- Relax Mood – Calming teas, soothing cocoa, and light bites to help you unwind.
- Extra Moods – Energetic and Focus options for those who want a boost or need concentration.
- Music Integration: Alongside your menu, handpicked YouTube playlists play on the right side of the screen, creating a complete sensory journey. From upbeat tracks to calming melodies, the music enhances the café’s immersive experience.
- Customer-Centric Design: The café’s digital interface is simple, stylish, and engaging—making it easy for customers to explore, enjoy, and connect with their emotions through food and music.
